Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian baked dish made of layered wide, flat pasta sheets with fillings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Hotdog
  • Quick-melt cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup Butter or Margarine
  • 1/2 cup All-purpose Flour
  • 4 cups Milk (evaporated or fresh)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

Prepare the Meat Sauce
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef (and/or pork) and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. Pour in crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce, banana ketchup (if using), sugar, fish sauce/soy sauce, and liver spread (if using).
  5. Simmer for at least 30 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Add the sliced hotdogs during the last 10 minutes of simmering.
  7. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
Prepare the Béchamel
  1. In a separate saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat.
  2. Add flour and whisk continuously until a smooth roux forms.
  3. Gradually pour in milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and chicken bouillon (if using).
  5. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens.
  6. Remove from heat.
Assemble the Lasagna
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them according to package directions. If using no-boil noodles, proceed directly to assembly.
  3. Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the dish.
  5. Layer lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce over the noodles.
  7. Spread a layer of béchamel sauce over the meat sauce.
  8. Sprinkle a generous amount of quick-melt, mozzarella, and cheddar cheeses.
  9. Repeat layers: noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese.
  10. Finish with a top layer of noodles, béchamel sauce, and a generous amount of the mixed cheeses.
Bake the Lasagna
  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  2. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.
  3. Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden brown.
  4. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. This allows the layers to set.
